Frequently Asked Questions

What makes the BanoGeniuss opportunity unique?

Industry exposure is important for teenagers, but it is hard to secure an internship at a young age. Even if they get an internship, learning is limited because senior people are too busy to train teenagers. This opportunity combines structured learning in industry-relevant skills along with industry exposure, resulting in every teenager working on a real company challenge with the guidance that they need .

What age group are the Courses for?

Ages 9 – 19. That’s usually 5th standard – 12th standard/degree college students Placement courses for Final Year students to secure career Entrepreneurship Residential Programme : Graduated Students & Working Professionals .

Who are your faculty/teachers?

All our faculty are selected based on 3 criteria: (i) they have practical experience running their own businesses or working in MNCs (ii) they are young and connect well with teenagers (iii) they are highly rated by students. If their rating falls below 8/10, they can no longer take sessions All faculty undergo rigorous training in our program and curriculum before being allowed to take sessions. Meet our faculty here ( link to homepage video) .

What is the validity period of the programs?

A single program is valid for 6 months. In a 3-course pack, students can complete the programs any time in the next 9 months. In the BanoGeniuss club, they can complete the programs in the next 24 months i.e. 2 years. .

What certificate do I get at the end?

You will receive a joint certificate from BanoGeniuss and the industry partner stating that you have completed your BanoGeniuss in the particular specialisation, specifying that you have solved a company challenge. .
