Learning Philosophy at BanoGeniuss

01

Build strong business fundamentals

We prioritize depth over breadth, our curriculum is curated by startup experts to build a deep understanding of core concepts, taught via cutting edge case studies, simulations and real-world capstone projects.

02

Apply, Iterate & Perfect

All business concepts learnt are applied to real world contexts via hackathons, live projects with startups, short 1-day challenges while building small businesses. We believe it is important for candidates to try, fail, get feedback (from CXOs) and iterate on solutions to truly grasp concepts.

03

Outcome backwards always

We work industry backwards while designing our courses, and work with students to understand their background, experiences, skills and goals. We tailor a learning experience exactly for those needs, and craft a customized roadmap that helps everyone stay on track to achieving the end result.

04

Become a part of the startup ecosystem

The best way to learn skills that are important in the real world is to truly immerse oneself in an ecosystem. To develop startup leaders we immerse each of our candidate in Bangalore’s startup ecosystem: Visit startups, network with VCs, learn from startup CXOs/practitioners.

05

Develop into an honest and brave leader

Success in the business world requires more than just technical skills and business knowledge. At Mesa, we focus on developing well rounded individuals by having them go through extensive workshops that develop personality, emotional intelligence/awareness and empathy.

Unique hands-on Learning

Unique hands-on Learning

In-Class Learning

The course features lectures (20, 10, or 5 hrs) using cutting-edge cases and hands-on challenges for feedback from startup experts. It also includes a graded capstone group project to enhance the immersive experience.

Skill Building Bootcamps

Master in Demand Tech tools like Figma and Tableau through project-based, hands-on learning in fun, engaging bootcamps.

Build your own business (BYOB)

Students form groups of 4, are given a 1 lakh budget to pick a problem area of their choice and build a small business from scratch. Students build a different micro-business every term (first 4 terms) and grades are judged based on final business metrics achieved.

Startup Accelerator

In the last 3 terms of the program, groups of 4-5 students build a startup, guided through research, idea finalization, MVP building, GTM, fundraising. Students can choose to continue the project after the program.

CXO Connects and Personalized Mentorship

Learn from the Stalwarts themselves! Gain insights from startup leaders through engaging discussions and personalized guidance through 1:1 chats.

Immersive Hackathons

Spend 2 days solving a real problem for a real startup. Work in groups and present your work to CXOs of the protagonist company. Get valuable feedback.

Career Acceleration Series

An array of well structured exit role specific Career prep elements that make students productive from Day 0 on their post-Mesa Career paths!

Workshops for Personality Development

Students participate in 1-day immersive experiences hosted by experts in sports, the military, arts, and more. These sessions aim to build confidence and a winning mindset in a light, fun atmosphere.

Student Led Activities

Peer-to-Peer learning is highly encouraged at Mesa. Clubbed with Networking Dinners,and Recreational Activities - students will get a flavor of Learning with fun from the Best minds of the Industry and their peers!
