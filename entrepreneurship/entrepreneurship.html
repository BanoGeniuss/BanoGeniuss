FAQ's

Do you provide a degree or a diploma?

No, we do not provide a degree or a diploma. Instead, we equip you with the skills, connections, and job opportunities necessary to develop a successful career as a startup leader or future founder.

Do you guarantee placements?

BanoGeniuss’s immersive training programs provide individuals with the skills, connections, and job opportunities necessary to develop a successful career in the tech industry. Our industry-led curriculum and personalised mentorship enable individuals to acquire the skills and experience required to secure top tech jobs. However, success ultimately depends on an individual’s hard work and dedication to their goals, supported by BanoGeniuss’s career assistance resources.

What kind of jobs will candidates get at the end of the program?

We focus on top startup jobs, including roles such as Founder’s Office, Entrepreneur-In-Residence (EIR), Product Management (PM), P&L Leaders, and Special Projects Leaders, among others. These roles typically offer an average CTC of over 20L and provide a platform for growth and development as a startup leader or founder.

Do we get to choose our 1:1 mentor from the industry?

Once we understand a student’s background and aspirations, we connect them with an industry expert who has followed a similar path. If a mentor is not found to be useful, we will find another one. Our goal is to provide students with the guidance necessary to succeed as a leader in the startup ecosystem.

Why is this program in-person and not online?

We believe that extensive, transformational programs require in-person contact hours. Although our program also includes asynchronous learning, all synchronous learning occurs in-person in a vibrant startup community and environment in Bengaluru.

What does it mean when you say the prgram is "personalized"?

We work with students to create a target list of 20 companies/roles based on their experiences, skills, and background. We then tailor learning pathways and exercises to prepare them for these jobs, and personally reach out to recruiters at these companies to secure job interviews.

What kind of support can students expect from the founders?

We have kept our founding cohort extremely small, with only 60 students. Each of these students will receive personal attention from both founders, who will help them develop market-relevant skills, connect them with appropriate mentors, and help them secure their dream job.

How do I apply?

Our application process will go live soon! Register to attend an info session with us, and we will keep you updated as soon as the application link goes live!
