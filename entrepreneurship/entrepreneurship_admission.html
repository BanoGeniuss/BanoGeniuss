Startup Leadership

Launching India’s First PG Program in Startup Leadership, starting in Sept’ 2023. Founding cohort will consist of only 60 outstanding individuals.
Our application process is designed to help us uncover your hidden potential to do something grand. Through the entire process, we will interact with you deeply, making it very personalised, while keeping it rigorous and extensive.

Eligibility Criterion & Key Admission Sections

Education

We encourage students from all UG discipline backgrounds to apply.

Professional Experience

It is ideal to have a minimum of 6 months to 6 years of work experience.

Key Admission Sections

Personality Assessment

40 minute online exercises to help us understand your true (& maybe hidden yet) potential as a future startup leader. No preparation is needed for this, as it has psychometric and basic reasoning components only.

Supplemental Test Scores

Feel free to submit (if you have), lifetime scores from any of the following exams GMAT, GRE, CAT, NMAT, XAT. Don’t worry if you don’t have or feel your scores are not competitive enough. Our process is designed to be holistic to know the real you.

Essay

1 short written essay, up to 500 words. Question to answer - “In your life so far, what is that one thing you have done or achieved which you are most proud of? And why?“

Interviews

Shortlisted candidates will have an in-person interaction with our admission panel, which will include leaders from the startup ecosystem.

Admission Process

Fill Out The Online Application - Who Are You?

Submit the following:
  • Personal & professional details
  • 1 Short Essay - < 500 words
  • Resume - any format; we leave it to you to fit the best parts in 1 page
  • Personality Assessment - 30 minutes online exercises. No preparation needed.
  • Supplemental Test Scores- submit if you have, lifetime scores from any listed exam

Shortlist & In-Person Interview - How Does Mesa Fit In Your Journey?

This will help us better understand your aspirations, and the value you can bring to the founding cohort and your peers Within 15 days of submission, the admission committee will carefully review all applications and share the shortlist for the next stage.
Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an in-person interview

Final Admission Decision

The admission committee will announce final admission results within one week
Accepted: Selected students will be offered admission to Mesa School of Business along with final program fee structure & scholarship decision.
Rejected: Candidates who could not get admission this year are encouraged to apply again in the following year
Waitlisted: Select individuals could be waitlisted, if any slots open up, admission committee will review and roll out offers accordingly

Scholarships

Our founding class will be made of exceptional, promising candidates and we are keen to remove all financial barriers for these right candidates, to also elevate the quality of the peer learning experience. We have more than 40 spots with scholarships ranging from 40% - 90% of the programme fee depending on the profile of the students.
Under Dog/Black Horse:

If you’re a hustler with no big brand names to back you, just your grit and determination to make it big.

Startup Pride Scholarship

If you’re an out and proud person who identifies as LGBTQ+, and find themselves passionate about the startup ecosystem.

Lifelong Learner Scholarship

If you have significant years of work experience (>6 years), and are looking to upskill to better equip yourself to be an effective startup- leader.

General Merit Based Scholarship

Candidates with exceptional academic records or test scores or professional accomplishments.

Emerging Leader Scholarship

If you’re fresh into your career (less than 2 years of total experience), yet have shown exceptional leadership qualities.

Athlete To Executive Scholarship

If you have played competitive sports (at least state-level), and are looking to transition into high paced startup leadership roles.

Trailblaz-Her Scholarship

If you’re a woman who has shown exceptional leadership abilities in the past in a job or as a founder.

Sherdil Scholarship

If you’re a veteran ( <10 years experience) looking to transition into leadership roles at startups or start on your own.

Himalayan Scholar

If you belong to the hill-states of India (HP, UK, J&K, North-Eastern States) and are looking to work in high-paced startups in a fast-track leadership role.

Bob The Builder Scholarship

If you’re a founder who has faced unfortunate challenges with their startup in the past, but that hasn’t dampened your spirit

Bharat Builder Scholarship

If you have experience building India at the grassroots level in non-profit/impact organisations.

Science In Business Scholarship

If you’re a scientist/ researcher with the knack to solve complex business problems.
