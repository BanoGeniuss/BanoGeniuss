Business Design

Uncover the big moves you need to make to achieve sustainable
10x growth

Our approach to Business Design

We have a structured 5-step process to help you define the right “10x” operating and financial shape. Create a detailed “product-market
fingerprint”, define sharp objectives and key results (OKRs) and get your resource allocation right to get the job done.

How we partner with you

Target shape (operating and financial)

Define your three-year out financial goals and
broad levers to get there.

Product-market fingerprint

Develop a deep understanding of what’s working and not working with customers in your addressable market through analysis – inside-out (funnel analysis by cohort, product teardown) and outside-in (customer interviews, benchmarking, industry reports)

Big moves

Assess & prioritize opportunities on size and fit with your company’s capabilities

Jobs to be done

Define the initiatives required or OKRs (Objectives and Key Results) to achieve the strategic goals

Resource allocation

Line up the right org structure, teams and capital budgets behind the key initiatives
