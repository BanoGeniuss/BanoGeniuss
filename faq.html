Frequently Asked Question's

What makes the BanoGeniuss opportunity unique?

Industry exposure is important for teenagers, but it is hard to secure an internship at a young age. Even if they get an internship, learning is limited because senior people are too busy to train teenagers. This opportunity combines structured learning in industry-relevant skills along with industry exposure, resulting in every teenager working on a real company challenge with the guidance that they need.

What age group are the Courses for?

Ages 9 – 19. That’s usually 5th standard – 12th standard/degree college students Placement courses for Final Year students to secure career Entrepreneurship Residential Programme : Graduated Students & Working Professionals .

Who are your faculty/teachers?

All our faculty are selected based on 3 criteria: (i) they have practical experience running their own businesses or working in MNCs (ii) they are young and connect well with teenagers (iii) they are highly rated by students. If their rating falls below 8/10, they can no longer take sessions All faculty undergo rigorous training in our program and curriculum before being allowed to take sessions. Meet our faculty here ( link to homepage video)

What is the validity period of the programs?

OA single program is valid for 6 months. In a 3-course pack, students can complete the programs any time in the next 9 months. In the BanoGeniuss club, they can complete the programs in the next 24 months i.e. 2 years.

Is the certificate recognised by universities?

The brands we have partnered with are leading global brands. The certification is immediately recognised anywhere. We also have a growing list of universities which are giving BanoGeniuss students preference in their admissions process. More importantly, the student’s experience and achievements through the project give them strong material to talk about in their essays and interviews – helping them stand out in the application process. We have had many students get admissions into Top universities and even get scholarships on the basis of their BanoGeniuss projects.

What is the duration of the JuniorCXO?

Each JuniorCXO is ranging between 10-15 hours. This is divided into 10-25 sessions of 1 hour each. Sessions are conducted from Monday – Friday over 9 weeks. Each Placement course is for 8 weeks. Weekdays(3 hours) and Weekends (4 hours each)

What is the age group of students in a batch?

The Junior batches have students from grades 5 – Final Year. The Senior batches have students who are placement oriented. Curriculum is different for every set of age-groups.

When are the batches?

Batches start on Mondays. Sessions run from Monday – Friday, 1 hour on each day. The exact timings and availability can be viewed in app once you subscribe the course.

Can I purchase now but start later?

Yes, we highly recommend this as we run limited batches for each program. You can view the batches which are available at www.banogeniuss.com Most parents choose to pre-book their seat to avoid missing out. Your purchase has a long validity (6 months for a single program, 12 months for a 3 pack and 2 years for Entrepreneurship) to give you complete flexibility.

What certificate do I get at the end?

You will receive a joint certificate from BanoGeniuss and the industry partner stating that you have completed your BanoGeniuss in the particular specialisation, specifying that you have solved a company challenge.

Is this an internship?

This is better than an internship! It’s hard to get internships at a young age, and company professionals don’t have the time to train young students. We bring you the best of both worlds – an actual company challenge, with structured training and guidance from our facilitators.

Will this help me get an internship?

JuniorCXO students stand out for their understanding of the business world and the projects they build, this gives them an edge when applying for internships. Having real world experience definitely strengthens the students CV!

Can I get a letter of recommendation from the companies?

When students need a LOR for internships/university admissions, we are able to provide these letters to the university/company directly. The letters are issued by BanoGeniuss since we have interacted with the student most closely in the sessions.

What happens if I miss a class?

If a student has missed a few classes for any reason, we will send over recordings to help the student catch up for those sessions.
